Welcome To Our Page

Welcome to Cornerstone Faith Soul Ministry — a Christ-centered ministry dedicated to prayer, care, and the proclamation of the Gospel. We exist to glorify God by gathering in prayer, visiting and counseling those in need, equipping church leaders for effective service, and spreading the Good News of Jesus Christ. Our vision is to see lives transformed, leaders strengthened, and communities renewed for God’s Kingdom. “Let’s make it real! – Cares to Share.”
