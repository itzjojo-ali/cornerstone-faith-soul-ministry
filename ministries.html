Visitation & Care

The Visitation & Care Ministry reaches out with compassion to those who are homebound, hospitalized, elderly, or experiencing difficult seasons of life. Through prayer, encouragement, and practical assistance, our team seeks to demonstrate Christ’s love in tangible ways and remind each person that they are valued, supported, and never alone.

×

Prayer Meetings

Our Prayer Meetings are a vital time of coming together as a church family to seek God’s presence, intercede for our community, and stand in faith for breakthrough. These gatherings provide an atmosphere of unity, worship, and encouragement, where we learn to depend on God and strengthen our walk with Him through corporate prayer.

×

Counseling

Our Counseling Ministry provides a safe and compassionate space for individuals, couples, and families to find guidance, healing, and encouragement through God’s Word. Whether facing personal struggles, relationship challenges, grief, or life transitions, our trained counselors walk alongside you with prayer, biblical wisdom, and confidential support, helping you experience restoration and hope in Christ.

×

Gospel Outreach

The Gospel Outreach Ministry is dedicated to sharing the message of Christ beyond the church walls. Through evangelism, community engagement, and service initiatives, we spread the hope of the gospel, reaching both local neighborhoods and wider communities. Our mission is to reflect God’s love and invite others into a life-changing relationship with Jesus.
