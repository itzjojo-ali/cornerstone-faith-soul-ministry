Our Mission & Vision

Mission

  • Gathering in prayer to seek God’s presence, power, and direction.
  • Visiting and caring for individuals and families with love, encouragement, and practical support.
  • Counseling and equipping church leaders for effective and Spirit-led ministry.
  • Offering biblical counseling that brings healing, restoration, and hope.
  • Proclaiming the Gospel faithfully to reach the lost, make disciples, and expand God’s Kingdom.
  • “Let’s make it real! – Cares to Share.”

Vision

Vision: To see lives and communities transformed through prayer, compassionate care, and the Gospel of Christ, while equipping and strengthening spiritual leaders to impact the world for God’s Kingdom.

Our Story

More than ten years ago, God placed a vision in the heart of Mr. Osea Batisaresare to establish a ministry that would help people through prayer, counseling, and the Gospel. What began as small gatherings of prayer and personal visitations has grown into a ministry that touches lives, strengthens leaders, and reaches communities with the Good News of Jesus Christ.

Value Statement

  • Prayer – depending fully on God’s presence and power in all we do.
  • Compassion – showing Christ’s love through care, encouragement, and service.
  • Integrity – walking in holiness, accountability, and faithfulness to God’s Word.
  • Servant Leadership – equipping and supporting leaders to serve with humility and excellence.
  • Evangelism & Discipleship – boldly proclaiming the Gospel and nurturing believers to maturity in Christ.
  • Community Impact – being a light that transforms lives, families, and societies for God’s glory.
